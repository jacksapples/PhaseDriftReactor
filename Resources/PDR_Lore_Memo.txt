EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=================

The Phase Drift Reactor (PDR) is a stationary, fuel-less electrical
generator that exploits a previously unknown property of the
planet's geomagnetic / informational substrate, which FICSIT
Phlogiston Division has provisionally named the "Coherence Field."

Pioneers in the field report that the PDR produces power in direct
proportion to the operational entropy of nearby machinery: a
factory floor with active conveyors, running splitters, smelters,
and constructors produces measurably MORE power than an idle or
empty one. The effect has been reproduced 312/312 times.

MECHANICS
=========

1. ENTROPY-TAP BASELINE
   The PDR samples the local coherence gradient within a
   configurable radius (default 96m). It counts active
   production buildings, moving conveyor belts, and engaged
   power consumers as "entropy events" per second.
   Baseline output is 50 MW at zero events. Each event adds
   0.5 MW. The PDR is throttled to its tier cap.

2. DARKNESS BONUS (UNDERGROUND)
   When the PDR has no direct line-of-sight to the open sky
   (i.e. it is enclosed, underground, or under a thick
   foundation), output is multiplied by 1.6x. Phlogiston
   Division hypothesizes that the open sky carries a
   "decoherence" component that the device partially cancels.

3. PHASE-LOCK SYNERGY
   When two or more PDRs are within 256m of each other and
   connected to the same power grid, they enter "phase lock."
   Locked reactors share entropy samples and stack their
   output multiplicatively: 2 PDRs = 1.45x combined, 3 PDRs
   = 1.85x, 4 PDRs = 2.20x (capped at 2.5x with 6+ units).
   This is the optimal endgame topology for high-MW factories.

4. TIER PROGRESSION
   The PDR ships in 4 variants, each unlocked through
   standard FICSIT hard-drive milestones:
     T0 "Coherence Tap"        50 MW cap  -- Hub Tier 4
     T1 "Phase Anchor"        200 MW cap  -- Hub Tier 6
     T2 "Drift Coil"          500 MW cap  -- Hub Tier 8
     T3 "Entropic Resonator" 1000 MW cap  -- AWESOME Shop
   The player may only build ONE tier at a time per PDR
   (tier is set in the build UI). Upgrading in-place
   requires demolition and rebuilding.

5. STABILITY PENALTY (ANTI-CHEESE)
   To prevent a "single PDR cheesing" strategy, the device
   enters a 30-minute cooldown if it is ever the SOLE
   producer on a grid. This resets if any non-PDR generator
   comes online. Pioneer feedback was clear: this prevents
   trivializing the power progression curve.

WARNING
=======
Phlogiston Division reminds all Pioneers that, in the event of
a PDR emitting an audible high-pitched whine lasting more than
90 seconds, the unit should be switched off and not approached
for at least 4 hours. There have been no reported incidents.
This warning is included per the FICSIT R&D Style Guide, Section
14.3, "Precautionary Language for Untested Devices."
